## 📋 Field Reference

### In-Stock Shows:
✅ Date/Time | ✅ Quantity | ✅ Supplier
✅ Invoice # | ✅ Truck # | ✅ Received By
✅ Location | ✅ View/Download Invoice

### Out-Stock Shows:
✅ Date/Time | ✅ Quantity | ✅ Issued By
✅ Received By | ✅ Department | ✅ Reason

### Adjustment Shows:
✅ Date/Time | ✅ Adjusted By | ✅ From/To
✅ Quantity Changed | ✅ After Quantity | ✅ Reason

### Transfer Shows:
✅ Date/Time | ✅ From | ✅ To
✅ Quantity | ✅ Reason

---

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Green** = Coming In | **Orange** = Going Out
2. Invoices only available for In-Stock entries
3. Newest entries appear at top
4. Every action shows WHO did it and WHEN
5. Reasons required for all Out-Stock and Adjustments

---

## ✅ Checklist

When reviewing an item, verify:
- [ ] Current stock level
- [ ] Total received vs issued
- [ ] All in-stock entries have invoices (if applicable)
- [ ] All out-stock entries have reasons
- [ ] Adjustments show before/after quantities
- [ ] Transfers show from/to locations
