# Supplier Management - Quick Start Guide

## 📍 Where to Find Supplier Management

### Desktop View (PC)
- Look at the **left sidebar menu**
- Find **"Supplier Management"** with a users icon
- Click to expand and see submenu:
  - **Supplier List** - View all suppliers
  - **Supplier History** - View past transactions
  - **Supplier Reports** - Generate reports

### Mobile/Phone View
- Look at the **top header** 
- Tap the **hamburger menu icon** (☰)
- Find **"Supplier Management"** in the list
- Tap to open submenu

---

## 🎯 Key Features

### 1. Supplier List
- View all suppliers in a table
- See: Name, Contact, Items, Quantity, Transactions, Status
- Search and filter suppliers
- Add new supplier
- Export all suppliers to CSV

### 2. Supplier Details
- Click on any supplier to view details
- See full contact information
- View complete transaction history
- Export supplier data
- Print professional reports

### 3. Export Data
- **Export All** - Download all suppliers
- **Export Single** - Download one supplier with history
- **Print Report** - Print-ready format

---

## 📱 How to Use

### Add New Supplier
1. Go to Supplier List
2. Click "Add Supplier" button
3. Fill form: Name, Contact, Phone, Email, Address
4. Click "Add Supplier"

### View Supplier Details
1. Click on supplier name or eye icon
2. View all details and history
3. Scroll to see transactions

### Export Data
1. Click "Export All" for all suppliers
2. Click "Export Data" for single supplier
3. Click "Print Report" for print format
